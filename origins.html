India's Ancient Origins

Indus Valley Civilization

One of the world's oldest urban civilizations (3300–1300 BCE) with advanced city planning.

Vedic Period

The foundation of Hinduism and Indian culture through the Vedas (1500–500 BCE).

Maurya Empire

India's first major empire (322–185 BCE) under Emperor Ashoka, who spread Buddhism.

Mughal Era

Islamic empire (1526–1857) that built the Taj Mahal and blended Persian-Indian culture.
